Places that I've Visited And there Information

Jaipur: Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is a vibrant blend of royal heritage, colorful culture, and majestic architecture in Rajasthan, India.

Jaisalmer: Jaisalmer, often called the Golden City, is famed for its stunning yellow sandstone architecture, the majestic Jaisalmer Fort, and its enchanting Thar Desert landscapes.

Jodhpur: Jodhpur, known as the Blue City, is famous for its vibrant blue houses, the towering Mehrangarh Fort, and its rich history and royal heritage in the heart of Rajasthan.
